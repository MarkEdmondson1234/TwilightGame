# Cyclic Events

Recurring events that happen on a regular schedule throughout the game.

## Seasonal Events

The changing of the seasons. Each season has a special event:

| Season | Event | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Spring | Easter Bunny Visit | Egg hunt minigame |
| Midsummer | Bonfire | When the witches dance |
| Autumn | Harvest Festival | Celebration of the harvest |
| Midwinter | Solstice Festival | Winter celebration |

## The Travelling Salesman

**Character**: Mr Bernards

Mr Bernards visits the village periodically with his travelling shop.

**Benefits**:
- Stocks goods that are otherwise hard to come by in the village
- Gives you an extra good price for your wares

## The Book Club

**Host**: The Old Woman

The Old Woman hosts a book club every season, when some very bookish people drop by.

**Opportunities**:
- You can befriend the book club visitors
- Have them come live in the village
