# Cloud Saves

Keep your game progress safe and play on any device with Cloud Saves!

## What are Cloud Saves?

Cloud Saves automatically backs up your game progress to the internet. This means:

- **Never lose your progress** - Even if you clear your browser or use a different computer
- **Play anywhere** - Start on your laptop, continue on your tablet
- **Multiple save slots** - Keep up to 3 different saves per account

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Press **F1** to open Help
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Find the **Account** section
4. Click **Sign Up** and enter:
   - Your email address
   - A password (at least 6 characters)
5. That's it! Your account is ready

### Signing In

If you already have an account:

1. Press **F1** → **Settings** → **Account**
2. Click **Sign In**
3. Enter your email and password
4. You're in!

### Sign in with Google

For even easier access:

1. Press **F1** → **Settings** → **Account**
2. Click **Sign in with Google**
3. Choose your Google account
4. Done! No password to remember

### Play as Guest

Want to try the game without an account?

1. Click **Play as Guest**
2. Your progress saves locally in your browser
3. You can create an account later to enable cloud saves

**Note:** Guest progress is only saved in your current browser. Create an account to keep your save safe!

## Managing Your Saves

### Save Slots

You get **3 save slots** with your account. Each slot can hold a different character or playthrough.

### How Saving Works

- **Auto-save**: Your game saves to your browser automatically
- **Cloud sync**: Click the cloud icon in Settings to sync to the cloud
- **Load from cloud**: Your latest cloud save loads when you sign in on a new device

### Migrating Local Saves

If you've been playing as a guest and create an account:

1. Sign into your new account
2. You'll see a prompt: "Migrate local save?"
3. Click **Yes** to copy your guest progress to the cloud
4. Your progress is now safe!

## Troubleshooting

### "Not signed in" message

Make sure you've created an account or signed in. Check the Account section in Settings.

### Save not syncing

- Check your internet connection
- Click the cloud sync button in Settings to force a sync
- Wait a moment and try again

### Lost my password

Currently, password reset must be done through the Firebase console. Contact the game developer for help.

### Playing offline

The game works offline! Your progress saves locally and syncs to the cloud when you're back online.

## Privacy & Security

- **Your data is yours** - We only store your game saves
- **Secure storage** - Saves are stored in Google Cloud with encryption
- **No tracking** - We don't track your gameplay or sell your data
- **Delete anytime** - You can delete your account and all data from Settings

## Tips

1. **Sign in before playing** to ensure your progress syncs
2. **Check the cloud icon** in Settings to see your sync status
3. **Use Google sign-in** for the easiest experience
4. **Create an account early** so you don't lose guest progress

Enjoy your adventure across all your devices!
